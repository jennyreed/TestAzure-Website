About Us

FirstClass Interiors
Our core value is to make this world a better place to live.
FirstClass Interiors has been in business for over 15 years, reflecting our community's commitment and enthusiasm for our services.
Our goal is to provide a sustainable, natural, and futuristic interior that makes a space livable. Roman and Greek civilizations advanced
the Egyptian art of interior designing and accessorizing by celebrating civic pride through their invention of domed-roof public buildings.
For their homes, elaborate Greek wooden furniture featured intricate ivory and silver decoration while the Romans concentrated on
marrying beauty and comfort, with both civilizations home interiors designed to reflect wealth and social and political status.

Jeff Gate

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
FirstClass Interiors was founded by Jeff Gate. According to him, "a place without an interior is like a body without a soul." He wants to provide a sustainable, natural, and futuristic interior that makes a space livable.

Staff Members

Lucy Lee

Senior Designer
As a senior project designer, Lucy is involved with every phase of the design process including programming, space planning, schematic and design development, furniture and finish specification, and construction documentation. She also works with more junior designers in planning and production, and really enjoys the opportunity to mentor them to help further their careers.

James Martin

Senior Interior Designer
James has always known he would end up in the design industry. While attending school for architecture, he became fascinated with the interior environment and shifted his focus to interior design. Now, James has a decade of experience in education and corporate workplace design, ranging from small classroom additions to large high schools, higher education facilities, and corporate headquarters.

Robert Jackson

Senior Associate
Known for his expertise in workplace design, client relationships, and the management of national accounts, Robert brings a diverse background and a well-rounded body of knowledge to the management of corporate interior design and construction. He oversees all phases from strategy and design to move in, and he directs all matters related to team communications, client development, and consultant management.

Laura Fratis

Interior Design Coordinator
Laura takes pride in creating spaces that can have a positive effect on users, especially in healthcare settings. She finds inspiration from natural environments, biophilia, art, music, and animals—looking for ways to incorporate those attachments into good design.

Becky Hamilton

Facilities Planner
As a facilities planner, Becky focuses her work on providing comprehensive planning solutions for public agencies. Her passion lies in her understanding of the critical role public facilities play in communities–both socially and physically.
